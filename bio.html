Shriya Nevatia

Biography

I am a member of the Class of 2014 from Queensbury, New York. I am majoring in Mathematics & Computer Science and minoring in Entrepreneurial Leadership. Beyond my academic work, I have practiced various styles of dance (including Classical Indian Bharatnatyam, Bollywood, Ballet, Jazz, and Tap) since age 3. In high school, I was involved in music and visual art, along with educational activities including tutoring and leading workshops. I taught a classical Indian Dance class following the completion of my graduation performance, called an Arangetram.

In the summer of 2011, I taught middle school science with Breakthrough Cambridge for 8 weeks. In the summer of 2012, I was in Project Breaker, a design thinking and social entrepreneurship challenge founded by TED fellow Juliette LaMontagne. At Tufts, I am on the Tamasha fusion dance team and Tufts Association of South Asians executive board. In the fall of my freshmen year, I was Assistant Choreographer for the Over the Rainbow 2010 production. I was also Art Director for the 2011 TASA Culture Show and logo designer for the 2012 TASA Culture Show. I am currently a tour guide for Undergraduate Admissions. I am working on breaking in to the Boston Startup scene and picking up design, business, and web development skills in this quest. I would like to work in Educational Technology after graduation and be an Education Entrepreneur.
