ABOUT

I am President of Assembled, a professional social network for Tech students & professionals, based in Palo Alto, CA. I am also co-director of Spectra, an all-women's hackathon. I am passionate about communities and an active member & moderator of Dreamers // Doers and Ladies Storm Hackathons.

I completed a B.S. in Computer Science at Tufts University in 2015. I founded an organization for young women in technology called The Violet Society in 2013. I've also completed General Assembly's UX Design Course, Project Breaker's Design Thinking program, and Breakthrough Collaborative's Summer Teaching Residency.

I am a trained Bharatnatyam, Bollywood, Ballet, Jazz, and Contemporary dancer & choreographer. At Tufts, I was co-captain of Tufts Tamasha, an all-female Bollywood-fusion dance team, and a member for 3 years before that. I ran multiple fundraisers, designed our logo and online presence, and choreographed dances every year that I was on the team. Currently, I'm a certified BollyX fitness instructor.
