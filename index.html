ABOUT

I'm a new graduate of Tufts University and I majored in Computer Science. From 2013-2014 I learned UX design at General Assembly and had two internships in the field, at the Center for Engineering Education & Outreach at Tufts and Pegasystems. Additionally, from 2014-2015 I worked on design & front-end development for a couple of student startups. This summer I am learning iOS development at Make School in NYC. I am actively looking for a full-time job as an entry-level iOS Developer.

I am passionate about educational technology and new media. In summer 2011 I taught science to middle school students at Breakthrough Cambridge, and in summer 2012 I learned about Design Thinking with Project Breaker. I love creating social media content and exploring new technological tools. I also enjoy mentoring and teaching students about programming, design, technology, and math.

I am a trained Bharatnatyam, Bollywood, Ballet, Jazz, and Contemporary dancer & choreographer. At Tufts, I was co-captain of Tufts Tamasha all-female Bollywood-fusion dance team. I am Technology Chair of Desi Dance Network Inc. and a certified BollyX fitness instructor.
