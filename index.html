ABOUT

I'm President of TenXList based in Palo Alto, CA & a recent graduate of Tufts University with a B.S. in Computer Science. I am passionate about educational technology & I love trying out tech tools and products. I also enjoy mentoring and teaching students about programming, design, technology, and math. I founded an organization for young women in technology called The Violet Society in Fall of 2013.

I've supplemented my Tufts education with Make School's Summer Academy, General Assembly's UX Design Part-Time Course, and Project Breaker's Design Thinking program. I previously interned and worked at Pegasystems, Tufts Center for Engineering Education & Outreach, and Breakthrough Collaborative.

I am a trained Bharatnatyam, Bollywood, Ballet, Jazz, and Contemporary dancer & choreographer. At Tufts, I was co-captain of Tufts Tamasha (all-female Bollywood-fusion dance team). I am a certified BollyX fitness instructor.
