ABOUT

I'm a senior at Tufts University majoring in Computer Science. This summer I was a User Experience Design intern at Pegasystems in Cambridge, MA. Before that, in Summer 2013, I was a User Experience Design Research Intern at the Tufts Center for Engineering Education and Outreach. I am interested in every level of development and design-- User Experience Design, Graphic Design, Front-End Dev, Back-End Dev, and Mobile Dev. I founded an organization in Fall of 2013 called The Violet Society for undergraduate women who are interested in programming, entrepreneurship, or design. In addition, I am passionate about education and taught Science at Breakthrough Cambridge in Summer and Fall of 2011. I have also been a dancer and choreographer for over sixteen years (Bharatnatyam, Bollywood, Ballet, Tap, Jazz, and Contemporary) and I am currently co-captain of Tufts Tamasha, a competitive all-female Bollywood-Fusion dance team. In my spare time I love to do dance workouts like BollyX, Pound, and Masala Bhangra. I also enjoy reading technology blogs, books, and magazines.
